Chapter 2

Understanding Identity and Access Management


Identity and access management includes many important concepts that are tested on the CompTIA Security+ exam. Users claim an identity with a username and prove their identity by authenticating (such as with a password). They are then granted access to resources based on their proven identity. Chapter 2 covers various authentication concepts and methods, along with some basic security principals used to manage accounts.
